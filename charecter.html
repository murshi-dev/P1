Un Samayal Arayil is a Tamil romantic comedy film directed by Prakash Raj.Kalidasan (Prakash Raj), an archaeologist is a chronic bachelor and is a born gourmet. Gowri (Sneha) is a dubbing artiste, who loves cooking which she had picked from her mother. Due to a wrong call, they converse and soon realize their love for 'kutty dosa'. Food plays an important role but they are unable to express their feelings due to their shy nature, insecurity and misunderstandings crop up.

Insightful and inspiring, this intense family drama follows a father's journey and struggle to heal his handicapped son and motivate him towards an active and productive life. While his father has many dreams for his son in the field of academics, his son is deeply passionate about cricket and hopes to follow the example of his Idol, Mahendra Singh Dhoni, the captain of the Indian cricket team. Will his father come forward to assist him in transforming his life and help him achieve his goal?.
