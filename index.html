PRAKASH RAJ

Producer|Actor|Director

Born - March 26, 1964 in Udipi, Karnataka, India
Prakash Raj is an Indian film actor, film director and producer who is known for his works in the South Indian film industry, and a few Bollywood films.
After working in the Kannada television industry and Kannada cinema for a few years, he made his debut in Tamil cinema through Duet (1994) by K. Balachander.
In remembrance, he named his production company Duet Movies.
